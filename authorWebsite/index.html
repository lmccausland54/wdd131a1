Welcome to the home base of Revie Weaver's work. Rev writes blazing new narratives in cutting-edge sci-fi and breathtaking fantasy worlds. Rev started writing on a whim as an outlet during immensly difficult life challenges, often from the kitchen table. Stories came in flashes and dreams, coloring daily life and adding magic to the mundane. Writing is now a crucial part of Rev in every way, and she's thrilled to introduce you to her worlds.

Revie Weaver's writing explores complex themes, realtionships, and expansive world-building through multiple point-of-view storytelling. Her novels feature strong female voices, diverse casts of characters, thrill and suspense, and heart-achingly tender moments that pull you right into the world.
